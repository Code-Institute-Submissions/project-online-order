Who Are We



Sofia's Pizza is a pizza delivery chain in Singapore. Established in 1998, the brand has the mission to serve authentic Italian home style pizza right to your doorstep.

Only “Sofia's Pizza in-house” brand of whole-peeled Roma tomatoes from Italy are approved and used worldwide.
We farm and produce our tomato sauce in the northern part of Italy; and before shipping out for consumption, we go through an intensive quality control process to assure product quality.

With our commitment in serving fresh and quality ingredients and making Sofia's Pizza available and accessible every day to everyone in Singapore, our delivery service do just that.

How to Order

Log in with your account

Please note that delivery is only in Singapore.
Before you make your order, please log in with your email address and password.
If you are not yet a registered user, please create an account.


Choose your items

Browse through the menu and click on the "+/-" buttons to add/remove the items you want to your shopping cart.
Check your shopping cart to view the items that you have selected.


Checkout

When you are ready to confirm your order, click on the "Checkout" button and you will see the total amount due.


Payment Due

When you have confirmed the payment amount due, click on the "Pay" button to proceed with payment.
Please note that credit card payment is only accepted.
You may click on the "Cancel" button to cancel the checkout transaction and all items will be removed from your shopping cart.


Payment Submission

When you completed the payment details, click on the "Submit Payment" button to proceed with payment.
Upon successful payment, you can view your order under the "Order History" link.
You may click on the "Cancel" button to cancel the checkout transaction and all items will be removed from your shopping cart.
